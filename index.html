Why Donate Blood?

Blood donation is a selfless act that can make a life-saving difference. Every two seconds, someone in need requires blood. Your single donation can save up to three lives.

Find a Blood Drive

Who Can Donate?

Most people in good health, between the ages of 18 and 65, and weighing at least 110 pounds can donate blood. Check our eligibility guidelines for more details.

Check Eligibility

The Donation Process

The blood donation process is safe, simple, and takes about an hour. It includes registration, a brief health check, the donation itself, and a short recovery period with refreshments.
